How to Register in Sadiq E Safar (SDS) App

April 27, 2026
Jilani Tech Official
8 Min Read

Welcome to Sadiq E Safar! Tour Management Application. If you're a travel agent or tour operator looking to automate your business, the first step is getting your agency registered on our platform. Based on our latest video tutorial, we've compiled this detailed step-by-step guide to help you set up your account in minutes.

1 Step 1: Access the Registration Page

  • Open the Application: Launch the Sadiq E Safar app to reach the User Login screen 00:15 .
  • Find Register Link: Tap on the "Register Here" link located at the bottom of the screen 00:23 .
  • Get Started: Click the "Get Started" button to begin the onboarding process 00:32 .

2 Step 2: Email Verification

Security is our priority. We need to verify your email address before proceeding.

  • Enter Email: Provide your agency's official email address. Ensure this email is accessible as you will receive a verification code 00:40 .
  • OTP Verification: Click "Send OTP". Once you receive the code, enter it into the app. The system will automatically verify your email and move to the next step 00:57 .

3 Step 3: Fill in Basic Agency Details

Once verified, a registration form will appear. This information will be used for your agency profile and invoices 01:36 :

  • Personal Name: Enter your full name 01:44 .
  • Mobile Number: Provide a valid contact number for account recovery and notifications 01:48 .
  • Agency Name: Enter the official name of your travel agency 01:53 .
  • Agency Logo: Upload your business logo to appear on your professional reports 02:01 .
  • GST Number: Enter your GST details. This is optional; you can leave it blank if not applicable 02:10 .
  • Address & Location: Enter your agency’s physical address. After entering the Pin Code, the app will automatically fetch the City and State 02:16 .

Click "Next" to proceed to the security settings 02:36 .

4 Step 4: Create Security Credentials

  • Set Password: Create a strong password. It is recommended to store this privately 02:46 .
  • Confirm Password: Re-enter the password to ensure they match 03:00 .
Pro Tip: If you need to correct any previous information, you can use the Back button to return to the form without losing your progress 03:17 .

Click "Create Account & View Plans" to finalize your profile 03:10 .

5 Step 5: Select a Subscription Plan

After creating your account, you will be presented with our flexible subscription options 03:31 :

  • Free Plan: Get basic access to start exploring the platform 03:40 .
  • Premium Plans: Choose from our professional monthly options (e.g., ₹999 or ₹1999) to unlock advanced features like automated reminders and custom branding 03:40 .
  • Payment: If you choose a premium plan, you will be directed to a secure payment gateway to complete the transaction 04:05 .

Once the plan is selected, your account is ready! You can now start managing your tours and tourists more efficiently 03:58 .
