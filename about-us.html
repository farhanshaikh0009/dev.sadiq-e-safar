About

Sadiq E Safar is a complete travel management platform developed by Jilani Tech, built specifically to help travel agencies streamline and automate their day-to-day operations.

Our platform enables travel agencies to manage bookings, customer records, payments, reminders, and tour operations from one unified system.

With a fast and reliable system, clean and modern interface, and powerful automation tools, Sadiq E Safar helps travel businesses save time, reduce manual work, and focus more on delivering excellent service to their customers.

Whether it's organizing travel packages, handling client data, or managing payments, Sadiq E Safar provides a smart and efficient solution designed especially for modern travel agencies.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower travel agencies with simple, intelligent, and scalable tools that simplify business operations and improve customer experience.

We aim to help travel companies grow faster by providing technology that automates repetitive tasks, organizes information efficiently, and allows agencies to focus on what matters most – creating memorable journeys for their customers.
